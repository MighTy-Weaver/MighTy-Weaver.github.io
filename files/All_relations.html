In this survey, your task is to decide whether each Commonsense Knowledge Tuple is palusible or not. Each Commonsense Knowledge is presented in the form of ([head], [relation], [tail]), where each element in the triple is a sentence.

Below are some PLAUSIBLE examples:

(PersonX go to play the match, isBefore, PersonX win the match)

(PersonX drink water, isAfter, PersonX feel thirsty)

(PersonX vote for PersonY, oEffect, PersonY receive praise)

These are all plausible knowledges, meaning that the head and tail will happen in some particular circumstances following a specific relation. On the contrast, implausible means that the head and tail is NOT related in any circumstance under this relation.

Below are some IMPLAUSIBLE examples:

(PersonX go to play the match, isBefore, PersonY dance at home)

(PersonX drink water, isAfter, PersonY feel happy)

(PersonX vote for PersonY, oEffect, PersonY watch TV)


For every question below, you should choose whether each knowledge tuple is plausible or not with the knowledge you've learnt above, and the hints below:

1. The knowledge is machine-generated, thus please ignore grammar mistakes, focus ONLY on the main events in both head and tail.

2. If the tuple is meaningful in the way that it can happen in any circumstance, then it is plausible. Otherwise it's not
